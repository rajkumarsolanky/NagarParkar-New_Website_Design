Ancient Temples of Nagarparkar

Discover the spiritual heritage and architectural marvels

Historical Temples

Karoonjhar Temple

Built in the 14th century, this ancient Jain temple showcases intricate architectural details and spiritual significance. The temple stands as a testament to the rich cultural heritage of Nagarparkar.

  • Built: 14th Century
  • Architecture: Jain Style
  • Location: Karoonjhar Mountains

Viravah Temple

The Viravah Temple complex represents the spiritual and architectural grandeur of ancient times. Its intricate carvings and peaceful atmosphere make it a must-visit destination.

  • Historical Period: Ancient
  • Style: Traditional
  • Location: Viravah Region

Temple History & Culture

The temples of Nagarparkar represent a rich tapestry of cultural and religious heritage. These architectural marvels have stood the test of time, serving as spiritual centers and showcasing the remarkable craftsmanship of ancient builders.

The temples feature intricate stone carvings, detailed sculptures, and unique architectural elements that blend local and religious influences. Many of these temples were built using locally sourced materials, particularly the distinctive white marble from the Karoonjhar Mountains.

Visiting Information

  • Opening Hours: Sunrise to Sunset
  • Photography: Allowed
  • Dress Code: Modest Clothing Required
