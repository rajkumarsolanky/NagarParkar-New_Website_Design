Weather & Climate

Plan your visit with our climate guide

Seasonal Guide

Experience Nagarparkar throughout the year with our detailed seasonal weather guide

Winter (Oct-Feb)

Peak Tourist Season

  • Day: 15°C - 25°C
  • Night: 8°C - 15°C
  • Clear skies, mild days
  • Cool evening breeze
  • Perfect for mountain treks
  • Crystal clear visibility
  • Minimal chance of rain

Best hours: 9 AM - 4 PM for outdoor activities

Spring (Mar-Apr)

Comfortable Season

  • 20°C - 30°C
  • Pleasant mornings
  • Light breeze
  • Great for photography
  • Perfect for nature walks

Best time: 6 AM - 11 AM

Summer (May-Aug)

Peak Summer Season

  • 35°C - 45°C
  • Very hot days
  • High humidity possible
  • Indoor activities recommended
  • Sun protection essential

Safe hours: Before 8 AM or after 5 PM

Monsoon (Jul-Sep)

Green Season

  • Day: 30°C - 38°C
  • Night: 25°C - 30°C
  • Intermittent showers
  • High humidity (70-80%)
  • Lush green landscapes
  • Waterfall activities
  • Sudden weather changes

Safe hours: 10 AM - 3 PM (weather permitting)

Travel Tips

What to Pack

  • Light, breathable clothes
  • Wide-brimmed hat
  • UV protection sunglasses
  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Water bottle
  • Basic first aid kit

Best Times

  • Early morning visits in summer
  • Midday activities in winter
  • Evening tours in spring
  • Indoor activities during monsoon

Safety Measures

  • Stay hydrated
  • Use sun protection
  • Check weather updates
  • Carry appropriate gear
